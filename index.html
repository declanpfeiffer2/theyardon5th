Lynchburg's Smokehouse

The Yard On 5th was born from a simple belief — that great food and great nights belong together. We built our pit in the heart of downtown Lynchburg in 2024 and haven't looked back.

Every cut of meat is slow-smoked over hardwood. Every cocktail is crafted with intention. Every night is designed to leave a mark.

This is not a chain. This is not a formula. This is The Yard — and it's yours.
